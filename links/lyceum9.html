lyceum9

About

lyceum9 is a tool for searching and browsing the GLAUx corpus databases of classical texts on the 9front operating system.

Currently a work in progress.

  • Designed to be used with acme(1).
  • Written in C.

Features

  • Browse the GLAUx corpus texts.
  • Search Ancient Greek and Latin vocabulary.
